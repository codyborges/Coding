A little about the Man

Joseph James Rogan is an American podcaster, UFC color commentator, comedian, actor, and former television presenter. Rogan began his career in comedy in August 1988 in the Boston area.Joe was a commentator for the Ultimate Fighting Championship(UFC) which he started in the year 1997. He still to this day continues this journey. In 2001 he was the host of a very popular game show called "Fear Factor" which ran until 2006. The Joe Rogan Experience is a podcast hosted by the man himself, which is a juggernaught in the podcasting space. It launched on December 24, 2009, by Rogan and comedian Brian Redban, who was its co-host and producer until 2013 when he was replaced by Jamie Vernon.
